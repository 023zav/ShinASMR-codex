About & Attribution

ASMR Shinkansen models the Tokaido & San'yō corridor (Tokyo–Hakata, 35 stations). By default the clock is live Japan Standard Time: trains appear where the schedule puts them right now, and at night the network honestly sleeps.

The timetable is a faithful re-creation of real service patterns (Nozomi, Hikari, Kodama, Sakura, Mizuho), not licensed JR data; positions are schedule-driven, not live train tracking, and no delay information is shown.

Map data: © OpenStreetMap contributors, served through OpenFreeMap where available. City art is generated, stylized interpretation — not satellite imagery.
